What banks don't explain about mortgage insurance

Most Canadians don't realize what they're actually buying when they check "yes" to mortgage protection at the bank. Here's how it actually works.

Post-Claim Underwriting

Bank mortgage insurance uses "post-claim underwriting." They collect premiums for years, but only investigate your health history when a claim is made. By then, it's too late"”and denial rates are shockingly high.

The bank is the beneficiary

The payout goes to the lender, not your family. They're protecting their balance sheet"”not the people you love.

Your coverage shrinks

As you pay down your mortgage, coverage decreases"”but your premiums stay the same. You pay more for less every year.

Change lenders? Start over.

Refinance, switch banks, or move? Your coverage ends. You'll reapply older"”and pay higher rates.
