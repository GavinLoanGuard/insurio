Why This Matters

Your bank offered you insurance. Here's what they didn't explain.

When you signed your mortgage, someone probably mentioned "creditor insurance." But here's the thing: bank mortgage insurance protects the bank, not you.

Post-Claim Underwriting Risk

Bank mortgage insurance uses "post-claim underwriting." They collect premiums for years, but only investigate health history when a claim is made. By then, it's too late—and denial rates are shockingly high.

Bank owns it

The policy belongs to the bank. They're the beneficiary. The payout goes to them, not your family.

Coverage shrinks

As you pay down your mortgage, your coverage decreases—but your premiums stay the same.

Not portable

Switch lenders, refinance, or move? Your coverage ends. Start over at higher rates.

Fast, streamlined coverage.

Your age, health, and mortgage amount affect pricing differently with each insurer. We work directly with Assumption Life for fast, efficient processing and present you with the best options—saving you time and money.

Questions

Common questions

Is this the same price as bank insurance?
Often similar or less. The big difference is what you get: we shop 8+ carriers to find you the best rate, coverage you own, that doesn't decrease, with benefits paid to your family.
Do I need a medical exam?
Most policies are "simplified issue"—just health questions on the application. For larger amounts or certain health situations, basic tests may be needed.
What if I switch mortgage lenders?
Your coverage stays exactly the same. It's tied to you, not your mortgage. Refinance, move, switch banks—your protection doesn't change.
How long does approval take?
For simplified issue policies, often within 24-48 hours. More complex cases requiring medical review may take 2-4 weeks.
Why shop multiple carriers?
Each insurer prices risk differently based on your age, health, occupation, and lifestyle. By comparing 8+ carriers, we can often save you 15-30% compared to a single-carrier quote.
