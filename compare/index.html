Hidden Risk

Post-Claim Underwriting

This is what most people don't know about bank mortgage insurance.

Post-claim underwriting means the insurance company collects your premiums for years—but only investigates your health history when you actually make a claim.

If they find anything you didn't disclose (even unintentionally), they can deny the claim. By that point, it's too late.

What this means for you

You pay for years Premiums collected without any health verification
Claim triggers investigation Health history reviewed only when you need it most
Denial risk Family left without coverage when they need it
